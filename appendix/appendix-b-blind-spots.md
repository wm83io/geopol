## Purpose

The framework's analytical power is bounded by what it cannot see. This appendix documents the structural blind spots identified through Day 70 analysis, assigns each a confidence level (how much we are flying blind), and issues standing weekly probe directives to surface data that would force framework revision.

The directives are not news-monitoring tasks. They are targeted intelligence probes aimed at specific signals that would either validate or break key framework assumptions. Each probe should be run once per week minimum, more frequently if adjacent data suggests movement.

**Day 70 audit note:** The framework's central trajectory inverted (Fork A → Fork B leading) within 72 hours of v2.6 publication, exposing a Fearon-mechanism blind spot — limited kinetic exchange treated as monotonic escalation rather than as type-revelation reopening the bargaining range. Audit reorganizes Appendix B around the post-revelation deal architecture phase, integrating the game-theoretical apparatus (PA-gap, Schelling focal points, commitment devices, Slantchev feigning weakness, Weisiger dispositional reading, Powell shifting-power) as new BS layer.
