## Section B — Structural Revisions (v3.1 Core Changes)

### B1 — Layer 4 Revision: Israeli Influence Upgraded from External Spoiler to Principal-Adjacent Actor

**Current v3.0 framing (Section 4.3):** Netanyahu as structural spoiler — external actor capable of disrupting US-Iran diplomacy through unilateral kinetic action or public pressure. One of four factions competing for Trump's attention.

**Revised framing:**
Netanyahu operates as a **principal-adjacent actor with direct Trump access**, not an external ally whose preferences are occasionally decisive. The distinction is structural:

- External pressure requires Trump to consciously override his agents (Witkoff, Kushner, Rubio). Institutional resistance can absorb or delay it.
- Internal participation bypasses agent mediation entirely. Netanyahu-Trump direct calls reset the US negotiating position before agents are consulted, producing Witkoff public statements as lagging outputs of Netanyahu's prior-day framing.

**Evidence chain establishing the mechanism:**
1. Feb 11 Situation Room: Netanyahu hard sell on regime change shaped original war framing
2. Iron Dome UAE operational embedding: Israeli soldiers operating UAE air defense during active US-Iran conflict — operational integration, not coordination
3. Day 70 PM Beirut strike: conducted with US advance coordination inside an active ceasefire window
4. Day 73: Netanyahu CBS "you go in and take it out" + Sunday Trump call + Monday Witkoff dismantlement demand — three steps, 24 hours, one vector. Witkoff's public position reset without any evident US institutional filter.
5. Netanyahu daily Trump contact (noted in v3.0) is the structural mechanism; the Day 73-74 sequence is its clearest operational expression

**Historical analog:** Cheney-Bush Iraq 2002-2003. Hawkish principal-adjacent actor with dispositional certainty produces policy outputs that lock in before institutional alternatives are evaluated. Key risk: irreversibility. Netanyahu's dismantlement demand is now Witkoff's stated position — rolling it back requires Trump to explicitly override his own agent, which is politically costly regardless of private preferences. The MOU's 12-15 year moratorium (achievable) is now inaccessible without a public US climb-down.

**Structural implication for probability architecture:** Every fork probability that treats Witkoff/Kushner agent behavior as the operative US signal is conditionally unstable. The agents are less autonomous than v3.0 assumed. Principal-level signal (Netanyahu-Trump call tempo and content) is more predictive than agent public statements.

**Layer 4 faction architecture revision:**
- Retain four-faction structure (dealmaker / Israeli-aligned / holy-war / accelerationist)
- Add explicit sub-mechanism under Israeli-aligned: "Netanyahu Principal-Access Channel — bypasses US agent layer; resets US negotiating position at principal speed"
- Note that no other faction has a comparable mechanism; Hegseth, Vance, Rubio all operate through normal institutional channels

### B2 — New Structural Mechanism: Meta-Negotiation Regression

**Definition:** The 4th Oman round (May 11, 2026) is not negotiating MOU terms. It is negotiating whether nuclear talks should be concurrent with or sequential to war-ending — the identical sequencing question that produced the pre-war negotiating stalemate and was the structural predicate for the Feb 28 war. 74 days of conflict, $25B US cost, 14M bpd supply disruption: the structural bargaining impasse has returned to the May 2025 baseline.

**Mechanism:** Fearon-Slantchev information rents were exhausted by kinetics (both sides now know each other's types) but Bayesian updating ran in the wrong direction because Weisiger dispositional readings reframed each kinetic signal as confirming unlimited-objective threat rather than updating resolve estimates. Each US/Israeli strike confirmed to Iran that the US objective is regime change; each Iranian defiance confirmed to Netanyahu that Iran cannot be deterred. Type-revelation without convergence.

**Predictive implication:** Absent a third-party commitment device (Trump-Xi, Hajj), the Oman process defaults to the Hamas/Hezbollah indefinite-deferral equilibrium. The mechanism will produce a 5th, 6th, and 7th round with "constructive but difficult" language until a forcing function terminates the process or a guarantor provides a credible commitment device neither principal can provide for themselves.

**Add to:** Section 5 (Structural Dynamics and Mechanisms) as Section 5.18.

### B3 — New Structural Mechanism: Netanyahu Principal-Penetration Pattern

**Definition:** Netanyahu has a direct principal-access channel to Trump that bypasses the US agent layer (Witkoff, Kushner, Rubio) and resets the US negotiating position through direct phone calls. The Day 73-74 sequence is the clearest operational expression: Netanyahu CBS Sunday → Netanyahu-Trump call Sunday evening → Witkoff dismantlement demand Monday morning. Three steps, 24 hours, zero institutional mediation.

**Distinction from v3.0 Netanyahu framing:** v3.0 modeled Netanyahu as a structural spoiler (external). B3 establishes him as functionally internal to the US decision loop on Iran. The difference: external actors require Trump to consciously override his agents; internal actors produce outputs that bypass agents entirely.

**Competing channel framing:** The Trump-Xi summit is not merely a diplomatic optic opportunity — it is a competing principal-access channel. Xi is attempting to operate a comparable direct-principal mechanism (leader-to-leader) that pulls Trump toward a deal-announcing optic. Netanyahu and Xi are competing for primacy in Trump's decision space. This reframes the summit's analytical stakes: not "will China pressure Iran?" but "can Xi displace Netanyahu's Sunday-call framing before it locks into operational planning?"

**Add to:** Section 5 as Section 5.19.

### B4 — Layer 5 Revision: PA-Gap Inverted

**Current v3.0 framing (Section 3.5):** Layer 5 concern was agents (Witkoff/Kushner) overstating Iranian flexibility to Trump (Feb 28 misrepresentation pattern). Constraint: deal collapses because agents cannot deliver what they negotiated.

**Revised framing:** The PA-gap has inverted on the US side. Trump principal is now MORE hawkish than the MOU agents' framework. The inversion path: Netanyahu principal-penetration → Trump aligns with dismantlement → agents adopt principal's harder line. The original Layer 5 risk (agents overpromise, principal rejects) has been replaced by the inverse: principal escalates past agents, agents follow, negotiating space collapses from the US side.

**Structural implication:** Both PA-gaps are now resolved — in directions that widen the gap between the parties:
- US principal: physical HEU extraction, facility dismantlement
- Iranian principal: nuclear talks deferred, HEU transfer with return clause, compensation, Hormuz sovereignty

Per Putnam-Leventoglu-Tarar: this is not the standard involuntary-defection failure mode (agents can't deliver). It is the principal-override failure mode (principal makes agent position undeliverable by escalating past it). The Witkoff-Araghchi MOU negotiation is now a dead channel; what remains is principal-to-principal via intermediaries, which is a structurally different and slower process.

**Add to:** Section 3.5 as an addendum: "v3.1 update: PA-gap inversion."

### B6 — Israeli/US-Coordinated Strike Disaggregation

**Current v3.0 framing:** "Israeli unilateral conventional strike during MOU window" as a single probability entry.

**Revised framing:** Split into two structurally distinct variants:

**Variant A — Israeli unilateral (without US coordination):**
Trigger: Netanyahu coalition reads MOU as intolerable, acts without Trump endorsement. US institutional resistance (Rubio, military chain) is the binding constraint. Probability: 8-12% (lower — requires Netanyahu to act against Trump's preference, which is costly given the relationship).

**Variant B — US-Israeli coordinated extraction:**
Trigger: Oman talks fail to produce nuclear-sequencing movement post-Trump-Xi; Trump adopts "go in and take it out" as operational directive; coordinated HEU-extraction operation framed as "nonproliferation" rather than resumed bombing. This variant bypasses the Netanyahu-unilateral constraint because it has US principal endorsement. Probability: 12-18% (higher — Netanyahu-Trump alignment on physical HEU extraction is already stated; operational planning would require DOE nuclear expertise, Special Operations, or Israeli deep-strike capability with US intelligence integration). Combined: 20-30% (held from Day 74 sweep).

**Analytical implication:** These variants have different trigger conditions, different warning indicators, and different escalation trajectories. Variant A is a Fork B spoiler. Variant B is a Fork A variant with different legal and diplomatic framing — not a return to Epic Fury but a targeted operation with nonproliferation justification.

**Add new watch indicators:**
- DOE nuclear-expertise forward deployment signals

## Section F — Calibration Notes for v3.1

### F1 — Speed-of-Rotation Acknowledgment (extend from v3.0)
v3.0 Section 12 noted that "the framework predicts the paths available; underprices the speed at which the architecture rotates between them." v3.1 should extend this: the Netanyahu Principal-Penetration Pattern is a speed mechanism. A single Sunday evening phone call can reset the US negotiating position in 18 hours. Framework rotation speed is bounded not by institutional deliberation but by Netanyahu-Trump call frequency. Build explicit speed-of-rotation language into Section 12 for this mechanism.

### F2 — Determinism Creep Check (carry forward from Day 70 audit)
The Day 70 audit flagged determinism creep: "the architecture does not act; it constrains." v3.1 should reapply this check to the two new mechanisms (Meta-Negotiation Regression, Netanyahu Principal-Penetration). These mechanisms describe causal pathways, not inevitable outcomes. The architecture narrows options; principals choose from what remains. Mojtaba/IRGC coalition could still endorse nuclear concessions; Trump could still override Netanyahu's framing if Xi provides a compelling alternative. The mechanisms elevate probabilities; they do not determine outcomes.

The Netanyahu Principal-Penetration Pattern is constructed from Western (CBS, NYT, Reuters) sourcing. Iranian-side interpretation of the pattern — that US is fundamentally an Israeli proxy — maps directly onto their counter-proposal's compensation and sovereignty demands. Both the analytical mechanism (B3) and the Iranian response (counter-proposal content) are consistent; they are reading the same underlying dynamic from opposite sides. This is not bias confirmation; it is convergent signal from independent source ladders. Note in v3.1 Section 12.
