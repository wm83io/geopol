### 7.2 Forking Paths

**Fork B — Negotiated Off-Ramp (27-37%, 30-day leading scenario):**
Remains leading but under sustained downward pressure for the first time since Day 67. Maintenance conditions: Iranian response is substantive (not maximalist rejection); Israeli unilateral strike does not occur in 14-21 day window; Trump-Xi produces Chinese movement on enrichment-right.
