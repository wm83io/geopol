## 4. Framework Additions

### 4.1 "Epic Passage" — Legal-maneuver kinetic resumption variable

Not a one-off event; a structural mechanism. Administration is positioning to rebrand any kinetic resumption as a new operation, resetting the WPA 60-day clock. This creates a third option between "strike now" (Fork A) and "deal now" (Fork B): an indefinite frozen conflict with kinetic legal cover available on demand. Implications:

- WPA constraint becomes manageable by re-naming, not by political confrontation.
- Republican defection coalition (Collins, Curtis, Murkowski) must adapt to a moving target; AUMF would need to anticipate operation-rename rather than current operation only.
- Frozen conflict is not equilibrium but **suspended kinetics with optionality preserved**.
- Iran's planning horizon must price possible resumption under any rebranding event — incentive to extend Hormuz-closure leverage.

### 4.2 Iranian Hormuz vessel-restriction law

If parliament approves the proposed legal restrictions on Hormuz traffic, Iran formalizes the IRGC's de facto blockade in domestic law. This raises the political cost to any future Iranian concession on Hormuz reopening: a deal requiring strait reopening would now require parliamentary repeal, not just executive direction. Hardens the structural delay pattern; weakens Fork B (Vance-Russia off-ramp) further if HEU custody concession is bundled with strait reopening expectation.

### 4.3 Kharg Island storage saturation as compression vector

Day 64 framework tracked production grinding down at managed pace. Kharg saturation introduces a discrete near-term forced-cut event: when storage fills, exports stop irrespective of pricing. Compresses the strangulation timeline from gradual to step-function. Estimated impact at saturation: ~$170M/day revenue loss (Bessent).

## 7. Conclusion and Forking Analysis

### 7.1 Central thesis check

The base v2.0 framework's central thesis (constraint architecture closes available paths regardless of decision-maker preference; faction misalignment determines style and timing within these constraints) is **drifting, not breaking**. The pre-Ford-departure window closed without Fork A execution despite a four-vector convergence signal Day 64 read as pointing at Fork A. The architecture did not produce the strike; it produced a Ford departure plus a legal-maneuver innovation ("Epic Passage") plus a "terminated" certification. This is the constraint architecture functioning as modeled, just routing through a different exit. The exit chosen is Fork D over Fork A, preserving Fork A optionality via legal cover.
