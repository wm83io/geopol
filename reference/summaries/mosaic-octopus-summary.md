## Core Proposition

Iranian and Israeli military doctrines co-evolved since 1979. Each is engineered as a counter-mechanism to the other. The 2026 conflict is the first kinetic stress test at scale. Doctrine is the slow constraint underneath operational tempo; it cannot be revised on conflict-relevant timescales.

| Axis | Iran (Mosaic) | Israel (Octopus) |
|---|---|---|
| Architecture | Distributed, generational, survival-optimized | Hierarchical, window-optimized, ablation-oriented |
| Lethality | Dispersed across geography, proxies, civilian terrain | Concentrated at decision-node (leadership, C2, infrastructure) |
| Trade-off accepted | Operational efficiency → survivability | Survivability vs attrition → decisive timing |
| Audience cost locus | Internal (regime legitimacy) | External / electoral |
| Planning horizon | Decades; recovery is the strategy | Months-to-years; preventive logic dominates |

## Mosaic — Four Operational Principles

1. **Distributed lethality** — TEL batteries, dispersed launchers, "missile cities," 700+ IRGC Navy small craft across 30+ bases. No single strike removes more than a fraction of capability.
2. **Redundant command authorization** — provincial IRGC, Aerospace Force brigades, embedded advisors hold pre-delegated launch orders. Decapitation does not silence retaliation (validated April / October 2024).
3. **Civilian terrain integration** — Natanz, Fordow, Pickaxe Mountain, urban concealment, dual-use industrial sites. Raises political-legal cost of strikes; degrades adversary precision-strike calculus.
4. **Graduated horizontal escalation** — lateral pressure (theater, domain, actor) rather than vertical. 2019-2020 Abqaiq / tanker seizures / Ain al-Asad exemplify.

**Sub-doctrines:** Aerospace Force (A2/AD saturation; calibrated terminal effects; launch-decision autonomy at operational level), IRGC Navy (Hormuz swarm + ASCM redundancy + mine ambiguity + fait accompli blockade), Cyber (Stage 1 prepo / Stage 2 calibrated disruption / Stage 3 mass-casualty held in reserve as deterrent), Nuclear threshold strategy (preserve capability, withhold weaponization, preserve diplomatic optionality).

**Factional sub-cultures (load-bearing for L4 analysis):**
- **Quds Force** — forward-deployed, proxy-operationalized, Khamenei/Soleimani lineage. Under acute stress post-2024 Syria collapse and Hezbollah degradation.
- **Aerospace Force** — homeland deterrent, Khamenei-aligned, "Hatami Doctrine" of missiles-as-strategic-ceiling.
- **Artesh** — conventional, professional, Pahlavi-era culture; latent doctrinal hedge.

Day 76 finding: 5-precondition coalition (Ghalibaf, Jafari, Hassanzadeh) operating in consensus mode under crisis, not factional competition.

## Octopus — Three Operational Features

1. **Direct Iranian targeting** — breaks the prior convention that allowed proxies to be struck while Iranian territory was off-limits. Progression: Nov 2023 advisors, April 2024 Damascus consulate (Zahedi), July 2024 Haniyeh Tehran, Sept 2024 Nasrallah Beirut.
2. **Decapitation as escalation control** — premise: networks lose coordination faster than lethality. Confirmed for Hezbollah Oct-Nov 2024 response; qualified by preserved residual missile inventory.
3. **Multi-front simultaneity** — Iran direct, Hezbollah, Hamas remnant, Houthi SEAD, Syrian militias, West Bank. Operationally validated at 2024-2026 tempo; not tested at sustained high-tempo scaling.

**Companion doctrines:**
- **Begin Doctrine** — no nuclear adversary; act on threshold not weapon; unilateral execution acceptable. 2026 is the first full application against a state with retaliation capability orders of magnitude beyond Iraq/Syria.
- **Mabam (Campaign Between Wars)** — continuous below-threshold strike tempo; deniability as instrument; intelligence-driven targeting. 2026 opening campaign is mabam amplified to wartime intensity.
- **Dahiya Doctrine** — disproportionate force against civilian infrastructure of adversary-controlled areas; deterrence-by-punishment not denial. Drives ICC/ICJ political cost.
- **Layered air defense** (Iron Dome / David's Sling / Arrow 2 / Arrow 3 + US THAAD/Patriot) — saturation attack is the structural vulnerability; magazine depth (interceptors) is the contingent variable.
- **Mossad-IDF integration** — Caesarea + IDF SOD joint instrument; depends on civilian-military trust contingent on political stability.

## Reciprocal Engagement

**Mosaic vs Octopus mapping:**
- Distributed lethality counters decapitation.
- Redundant command counters senior-leadership strikes.
- Civilian terrain raises the political cost of acting on intelligence.
- Horizontal escalation counters concentrated force.

**Octopus vs Mosaic mapping:**
- Direct Iranian targeting inverts the asymmetric advantage of proxy distribution.
- Decapitation degrades network coordination even when capability survives.
- Multi-front simultaneity absorbs lateral-escalation options.

**Time-horizon collision** (the most consequential asymmetry): Iran can afford operational loss if the regime survives the planning horizon; Israel cannot afford operational loss because operational loss converts to existential pressure on the shorter Israeli planning horizon. This is the Powell shifting-power mechanism in doctrinal form. Fork D' (indefinite deferral) is doctrinally tolerable for Iran, doctrinally unsustainable for Israel.

**Audience-cost asymmetry:** Iran's win-set is tighter than Israel's win-set in any given crisis because publicly committed coalition speech locks Iranian positions; Israeli electoral pressure permits oscillation. Asymmetric PA-gap finding (Day 76) is doctrinally rooted, not contingent diplomatic mistake.

## 2026 Stress-Test Findings

**Iran:**
- Mosaic redundancy held under decapitation strike (architectural assumption validated).
- Proxy network now thinner than at any point since early 2000s; Quds Force doctrine has not yet visibly adapted.
- Threshold strategy survived kinetic pressure; HEU treated as bargaining asset.
- 5-precondition position publicly committed → diplomatic flexibility narrowed by design.

**Israel:**
- Octopus produced operational success but no political settlement (premise partially falsified at scale tested).
- Multi-front simultaneity scaled but encountered US supplier-network depletion (PAC-3, SM-3, AIM-120, Tomahawk).
- Layered air defense interception held; inventory drawdown approached saturation.
- Begin Doctrine binary framing (eliminated vs intact) does not specify action at the in-between Slantchev-feigning-weakness outcome the campaign produced. Decisions currently operate in a doctrinal gap.
- US principal channel no longer institutionally stable; doctrinal response is dual-track (Penetration via direct Trump contact + unilateral options).

## First-Mover Question (BS-15) — Doctrinal Framing

The framework's dominant opacity, in doctrinal terms, is which side's doctrine first encounters a stress-point it cannot absorb without revision.

- **Iran's binding stress-point** is internal: strangulation transmitted through Bazaari, Bonyad, Khatam al-Anbiya channels (BS-1b). Doctrine assumes absorbable; 2026 timeline may compress beyond absorption capacity.
- **Israel's binding stress-point** is external: US supplier-network depletion + A1 oscillation in the Trump principal channel that Begin/Octopus both assumed institutional stability would prevent.

The doctrinal layer is the slow constraint beneath SITREP-tempo decisions and will shape the post-2026 strategic environment regardless of which fork the present cycle consolidates.
